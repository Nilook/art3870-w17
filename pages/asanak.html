Asanak Book Design Project

Iranian indigenous cultures are unlimited sources for contemporary artistic creations. Among these, tribes culture has a special place. The concept of Qashqai tribe literature has such a strong connection to Iranian mythology and traditions that it is true to say that the only dissimilarity is the Turkish language.

The oldest poem form among Qashqai people is Asanak. Containing visual elements and expressing widespread concepts such as love, separation, and life makes Asanaks a potential basis for artworks. Asanak book design project is my BA thesis project in which I tried to visualize the spirit of poems.
